Date: June 2024 | Location: Houston, Texas, USA
Project Lead: Nur Mohammad Ali  |  12 Volunteers  |  32 Senior Citizens
Project Overview:
In June 2024, I led a volunteer team for Houston International Jaycees to organize a daylong tech skills training for 32 American senior citizens (age 60+) in Houston, Texas.

Our Goal: Help seniors confidently use modern technology to connect with distant relatives and friends.

What We Taught:
  • How to use ChatGPT for information and conversation
  • How to connect on Facebook
  • How to join Google Meet and Zoom calls with family and doctors
  • Basic smartphone & computer safety tips
Key Moments:
  • Friendly, step-by-step guidance
  • One-on-one device support for every senior
  • Group Q&A and fun tech games
  • Team of 12 dedicated volunteers from diverse backgrounds
Personal Impact:
It was inspiring to see seniors embrace new technology and reconnect with their loved ones. Many participants expressed new confidence and happiness at the end of the training day.
